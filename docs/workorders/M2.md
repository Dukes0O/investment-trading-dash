# M2 work order — validation, replay, and the short-side question

Issued 2026-07-22 by the architect (Fable 5) after M1 review and fixes
(commit `b3103f8`). Builder: Codex. Read `AGENTS.md` and `docs/PROJECT_SPEC.md`
before starting. Every experiment goes through `engine/registry/` including
failures. Do not touch the 18-month holdout.

## 1. Replay drill (required gate before Fall live)

Build `trendlab replay --weeks N` (default 52): for each of the last N
Fridays before the holdout-safe boundary of live operation (replay runs on
full data — it is an operational drill, not research), truncate data to that
decision date, run the full weekly pipeline (validate, states, stops,
sizing, actions document), and record the sequence of action documents plus
any failures to `engine/registry/manifests/replay-<id>.json`. Acceptance:

- Zero crashes; every halt has a stated data reason.
- Each week's action matches what the backtest's signal would have been for
  that week (this is the regression net for the week-exposure fix).
- A summary table of week-by-week states and actions, written to
  `engine/output/`.

## 2. Experiment: symmetric long/short 30-week baseline (Kyle's reversal question)

Hypothesis: participating in the downtrend after a break (short until the
weekly close reclaims the 30-week SMA) improves risk-adjusted results
versus going to cash. Test the exact mirror of the pre-registered baseline —
**zero new parameters**:

- Short entry: completed weekly close < 30-week SMA and 10-week SMA <
  30-week SMA. Cover: completed weekly close > 30-week SMA. Long side
  unchanged. Long/flat/short, never both.
- Same folds, costs, and registry protocol as the baseline; register as
  strategy `trend-30w-ls-v1` (status: research) and compare per fold against
  the long/flat baseline and buy-and-hold.
- Extend `simulate` to short positions: stops sit ABOVE entry; intrabar stop
  check uses bar.high; a gap through the stop fills at the open; borrow cost
  as a configurable bps/day placeholder (default 25 bps/yr) documented in
  the config, not hardcoded.
- Report the long and short legs' contributions separately. Expected prior
  (state it in the manifest): short side helps GLD/TLT more than SPY.

Do NOT build a faster weakening-detector in M2. If the zero-parameter mirror
shows an out-of-sample signal, a follow-up experiment with the 20–80 day
layer gets its own registered design and gates. Execution instruments
(outright short in margin vs inverse ETF in TFSA) are Kyle's decision at
promotion time; note the constraint in the strategy file, nothing more.

## 3. Portfolio heat aggregation (KNOWN LIMITATION in DECISIONS.md)

Sizing must count open-position risk: for each held position, risk = qty x
(current price − active stop), floored at zero; pass the sum as
`current_heat`. Source of truth for held positions and stops: the latest
engine actions plus `data/portfolio.json`. Add tests: a second BUY in the
same week and a BUY with existing open risk both respect the 1% cap.
